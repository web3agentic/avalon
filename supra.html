What is an Airdrop?

A cryptocurrency airdrop is a distribution of a cryptocurrency token or coin, usually for free, to numerous wallet addresses. Airdrops are primarily implemented as a way of gaining attention and new followers, resulting in a larger user base and a wider disbursement of coins.

The SUPRA Token

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the SUPRA Airdrop

The SUPRA Airdrop is a free distribution of SUPRA tokens ($SUPRA) to eligible participants. It's a way for the SUPRA project to reward early supporters, expand its community, and distribute tokens to potential users of the platform. Participants who meet certain criteria can claim a predetermined amount of $SUPRA tokens.

The SUPRA Airdrop is scheduled to launch on June 15, 2025. Registration begins on May 1, 2025 and will remain open for 30 days. After registration closes, the team will verify eligibility and distribute tokens within 14 days.

To participate in the SUPRA Airdrop, you need to:

  1. Register on the official SUPRA website
  2. Connect your cryptocurrency wallet (Ethereum-based)
  3. Complete KYC verification if required
  4. Meet the eligibility criteria (holding specific tokens or completing community tasks)
  5. Claim your tokens when distribution begins

The amount of $SUPRA tokens you receive depends on various factors, including your participation level, how early you register, and whether you complete additional community tasks. The base allocation is 1,000 $SUPRA tokens, with the possibility of earning up to 5,000 $SUPRA through additional activities.

There are no fees to register for the SUPRA Airdrop. However, when claiming your tokens, you will need to pay standard network transaction fees (gas fees) on the Ethereum network. The SUPRA team does not charge any additional fees for participation.

Tokens will be distributed within 14 days after the registration period ends. The exact distribution schedule will be announced on our official channels. Some tokens may be subject to a vesting schedule, where you receive a portion immediately and the remainder over a set period.

Yes, there is a partial lockup period for airdropped tokens. 30% of your tokens will be immediately available, while the remaining 70% will be released linearly over a 6-month period. This vesting schedule helps maintain price stability and encourages long-term participation in the ecosystem.
