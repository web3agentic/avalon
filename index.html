Frequently Asked Questions

What is Avalon?
Avalon is a next-generation blockchain infrastructure protocol that leverages a multi-layered architecture to provide unprecedented scalability, security, and interoperability for decentralized applications and services.
Who is eligible for the Avalon airdrop?
The Avalon airdrop is open to all crypto enthusiasts with a compatible Web3 wallet. To qualify, participants must complete the required social tasks and verify their wallet address through our official airdrop platform.
How many Avalon tokens will I receive?
Each eligible participant will receive a base allocation of 5,000 Avalon tokens. Additional tokens can be earned by completing bonus tasks and referring friends to the airdrop program.
When will I receive my Avalon tokens?
Avalon tokens will be distributed according to the vesting schedule after the Token Generation Event (TGE). The initial 20% will be released at TGE, with the remaining 80% distributed over the following 4 months (20% per month).
Is there a fee to participate in the airdrop?
No, participation in the Avalon airdrop is completely free. You only need to cover the standard network gas fees when connecting your wallet to verify your address. Beware of scams asking for payments or deposits.
Where can I trade Avalon tokens after distribution?
Avalon tokens will be listed on major centralized and decentralized exchanges shortly after the TGE. The official listing announcements will be made through our official communication channels.
How can I stay updated about Avalon?
Follow our official social media channels, join our community on Telegram and Discord, and subscribe to our newsletter to receive the latest updates about Avalon development, partnerships, and token events.
